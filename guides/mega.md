# How to remove your course from MEGA (mega.nz)

> Canonical version: [https://delister.co/remove/mega](https://delister.co/remove/mega). This repo mirrors it for reference and machine-readability.

When your course shows up on a piracy site, the download itself usually is not on that site. It sits on a file host like MEGA (mega.nz), and the listing just links to it. That works in your favor: MEGA is a compliant host with a designated copyright agent, so a notice sent to the right place removes the file, not just one link to it.

## Does MEGA honor DMCA notices?

Yes. MEGA is a mainstream host with a compliance obligation and a designated copyright agent, and it acts on valid notices. Taking the file down at the host is the highest-leverage move available, because it kills every listing that points to it at once. We have filed against MEGA-hosted course files ourselves, in July 2026, and the route below is the one that worked.

## The steps

### 1. Find the exact MEGA link

Open the pirate listing that ranks for your course name and find where the download points. On most listing sites the visible button leads to a mega.nz file or folder URL. Copy the whole link, including the part after the `#`, which is the decryption key MEGA needs to identify the file. If the listing hides the link behind a countdown or a shortener, follow it through until you land on the mega.nz address.

### 2. Send a DMCA notice to MEGA's copyright agent

Email `copyright@mega.io`, or use the copyright page linked from the mega.io footer, and include:

- Your full legal name and country as the rights holder.
- Your course name and a one-line description of the work.
- Your official sales page URL as the authorized example of the work.
- The exact infringing MEGA link or links. One notice can list several.
- The two sworn statements a DMCA notice requires: a good-faith belief that the use is not authorized, and that the information is accurate and you are the rights holder or authorized to act.

Send it from an address on your own domain where a reply can reach you.

### 3. Confirm the takedown

Re-open the link after MEGA acts. A file or folder that has been taken down no longer downloads: it reports a takedown or blocked state instead of serving the archive. Confirm at the MEGA link itself, not the listing page, because the listing can stay up while pointing at a dead file.

## Time cost

Around 20 minutes for a first notice, most of it spent finding the true link behind the listing. Then a recheck a few days later, and again whenever a reupload appears.
